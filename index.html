About Me

Where Technology
Meets Business

My name is Abdu Ramshad M K — a results-driven professional from Vaniyambalam, Malappuram, Kerala, holding dual degrees in BSc Information Technology (University of Calicut) and BBA (Bharathiar University, Coimbatore). This academic combination reflects my core philosophy: the most effective professionals speak both the language of technology and the language of business.

Currently, I serve as Branch Manager at myG India Pvt Ltd, one of Kerala's prominent consumer electronics retail organisations. I direct daily operations, lead a dedicated team, manage multi-category product sales spanning mobiles, laptops, and home appliances, and champion an exceptional after-sales customer experience that drives loyalty and sustained business growth.

My professional journey has been intentionally cross-disciplinary: I began as a Computer Faculty member, igniting a passion for education and programming. I then engineered enterprise IT infrastructure as a Network Engineer, before pivoting into field-based business development at Paytm. Each chapter added a layer of capability that informs every leadership decision I make today.

Beyond my corporate role, I actively practise as a freelance web developer — crafting responsive, performant websites and digital solutions for clients. I am perpetually curious about emerging technologies and genuinely believe the intersection of IT and commerce is where the most meaningful opportunities are created.
